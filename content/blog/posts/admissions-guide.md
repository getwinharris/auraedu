Aura Medical keeps admissions reachable: **no NEET required and no upper age bar** for the B.E.M.S. programme. Here is how the process works.

## 1. Enquire

Use the contact form or visit the campus at Saravanampatti, Coimbatore to request the prospectus and confirm the current eligibility for your chosen course.

## 2. Check eligibility

- **B.E.M.S.:** 10+2 with Biology (verify exact requirement with admissions).
- **M.D.E.H.:** B.E.M.S. or equivalent.
- **D.Acu:** 10+2; **M.Acu:** D.Acu or equivalent.
- **D.H.M. & C.T.:** 10+2.

## 3. Prepare documents

Keep these ready: 10th and 12th certificates, transfer certificate, conduct certificate, proof of residence, and recent passport-size photographs.

## 4. Confirm your seat

The admissions desk reviews your documents and confirms your seat. Hostel and placement support are available for outstation and local students.

Have questions? Reach the admissions desk through the contact page.
