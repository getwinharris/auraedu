Planetary transits are the movement of planets through the zodiac signs in real time. Unlike your birth chart which is fixed, transits show the current cosmic weather affecting everyone. 2026 brings several significant transits that will shape global and personal events.

## Key Transits of 2026

### Saturn in Pisces (Meena) — Until March 2027
Saturn continues its transit through Pisces, having entered this sign in 2025. This transit brings:
- **Structure to spirituality**: Organized spiritual practice yields deep results
- **Creative discipline**: Artists and creators find structured success
- **Health focus**: Immune system and feet need attention
- **Water sign focus**: Cancer, Scorpio, and Pisces feel Saturn's effects most strongly

### Jupiter in Taurus (Vrishabha) — Until May 2026
Jupiter's transit through Taurus brings:
- **Financial expansion**: Opportunities for stable, long-term wealth building
- **Relationship growth**: Commitment and deepening of bonds
- **Earth signs benefit**: Taurus, Virgo, and Capricorn experience growth
- **Agricultural abundance**: Positive period for farming and food industries

### Jupiter in Gemini (Mithuna) — From May 2026
When Jupiter moves into Gemini, the energy shifts:
- **Communication expands**: Learning, teaching, and writing flourish
- **Business networking**: Connections and partnerships bring opportunities
- **Air signs benefit**: Gemini, Libra, and Aquarius see new doors open
- **Short travel**: Multiple short trips bring professional benefits

### Rahu and Ketu Axis — Throughout 2026
Rahu in Gemini and Ketu in Sagittarius continues:
- **Technology focus**: Digital transformation and AI advancement accelerate
- **Education revolution**: Online learning and alternative education grow
- **Philosophical shifts**: Spiritual seekers question traditional structures
- **Travel industry**: Recovery and transformation of travel sector

### Mars Transits
- **Mars in Cancer** (Jan-Feb): Emotional sensitivity in actions — avoid impulsive decisions
- **Mars in Leo** (Feb-Apr): Bold action — excellent for creative projects
- **Mars in Virgo** (Apr-Jun): Detailed execution — perfect for planning
- **Mars in Libra** (Jun-Aug): Partnership focus — collaborative work excels

## Transit Effects by Element Group

### Fire Signs (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius)
2026 brings career focus and leadership opportunities. Saturn's aspect from Pisces challenges you to build sustainable structures. Jupiter's transit through Gemini after May enhances learning and communication.

### Earth Signs (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn)
Financial stability and relationship growth are highlights. Jupiter's transit through Taurus (until May) directly benefits you. Use this period for long-term planning and investment.

### Air Signs (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius)
Rahu's presence in Gemini amplifies your innovative thinking. After May, Jupiter joins Rahu in Gemini, creating a powerful conjunction for communication, learning, and networking success.

### Water Signs (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces)
Saturn's transit through Pisces asks for discipline and structure. While this may feel restrictive, it's building foundations for lasting success. Emotional maturity gained now will serve you for years.
