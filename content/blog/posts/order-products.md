## Add products

Open **Shop**, use the plus and minus controls to set quantities, then open the cart. Reducing an item to zero removes it.

## Choose delivery

At checkout, select your default or another saved address. You can also enter a different delivery address for this order and optionally save it with a name such as Home or Office.

## Pay and track

Complete the live Razorpay payment. Your confirmed order appears under **Dashboard > My Orders** with delivery status.
