## Product payments

Product orders use the secure live payment option shown at checkout. Review the amount and delivery address before approving payment.

## After payment

The website verifies the payment before creating the order. Do not repeat payment if you receive a reference but the page is delayed. Check **My Orders** first, then contact support with the payment reference.
