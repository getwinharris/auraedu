Acupuncture is an established needle-based therapy that Aura Medical teaches as part of its allied-health training. Two programmes are offered:

## D.Acu — Diploma in Acupuncture

A focused programme covering meridian theory, point location, needling technique, and supervised clinic practice. It is suited to learners who want a strong practical foundation.

## M.Acu — Master in Acupuncture

An advanced programme for practitioners who already hold a D.Acu or equivalent, going deeper into meridian diagnosis and integrated therapy planning.

## How training works

Both programmes pair **theory with supervised clinical practice**, so students learn needling in a controlled setting before working with patients. Confirm the current duration and eligibility with the admissions desk.

## Who it is for

D.Acu is generally open to 10+2 holders; M.Acu builds on prior acupuncture training. Enquire through the contact form for the right pathway.
