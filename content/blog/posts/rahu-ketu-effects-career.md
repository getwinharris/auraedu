**Rahu** and **Ketu** are often called shadow planets or the lunar nodes. In Vedic astrology, they hold immense significance — representing your karmic past (Ketu) and your karmic future (Rahu). Their influence on your career can be profound and life-changing.

## Understanding Rahu and Ketu

- **Rahu** (North Node): Represents worldly desires, ambition, obsession, and material success. It drives you toward new experiences and unfamiliar territory.
- **Ketu** (South Node): Represents detachment, spirituality, past-life skills, and letting go. It pulls you away from material attachments toward liberation.

## Rahu in Career Houses

### Rahu in 1st House
You have a magnetic personality and ambitious drive. You may change careers multiple times, always seeking greater recognition. Leadership roles in technology, media, or politics suit you.

### Rahu in 2nd House
Financial ambition is strong. You may earn money through unconventional means — digital businesses, foreign investments, or speculative ventures. Be careful with overspending.

### Rahu in 5th House
Creative careers, stock trading, and speculative businesses are favored. You may excel in entertainment, sports, or education technology.

### Rahu in 7th House
Partnerships drive your career. You may succeed through joint ventures, business partnerships, or public-facing roles. International business and consulting are favorable.

### Rahu in 10th House
One of the most powerful placements for career success. You can achieve significant fame, authority, and recognition. Politics, corporate leadership, and innovative industries are ideal.

### Rahu in 11th House
Strong financial gains through networks and social circles. You excel in sales, marketing, social media, and large organizations.

## Ketu in Career Houses

### Ketu in 1st House
You may feel pulled toward spirituality rather than material success. Research, analysis, and behind-the-scenes roles suit you. You have natural investigative abilities.

### Ketu in 4th House
Real estate, agriculture, and property management may feature in your career. You prefer working from home or in peaceful environments.

### Ketu in 10th House
You may experience ups and downs in your career. Your path involves service-oriented professions, spiritual work, or healing arts. Success comes through humility.

## Remedies for Rahu-Ketu Challenges

- **Chant Rahu mantra**: "Om Rahave Namah" (108 times, especially on Saturdays)
- **Chant Ketu mantra**: "Om Ketave Namah" (108 times, especially on Tuesdays)
- **Wear** a 7-faced Hessonite (Gomed) for Rahu or Cat's Eye (Vaidooryam) for Ketu after expert consultation
- **Donate** blue cloth, blankets, or coconuts on Saturdays
- **Feed** stray animals and birds regularly

## Conclusion

Rahu and Ketu are your karmic guides — Rahu shows where you need to grow, and Ketu shows where you've already been. By understanding their placement in your birth chart, you can align your career choices with your soul's journey.
