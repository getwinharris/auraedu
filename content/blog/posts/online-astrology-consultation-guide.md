Online astrology consultations make Vedic guidance accessible wherever you live. Preparing your details and questions before the appointment helps you use the scheduled time well.

## Before Your Consultation

### Gather Your Birth Details

Bring your date, time, and place of birth. If your exact birth time is unavailable, mention that in your booking notes so the consultant can prepare an appropriate approach.

### Prepare Your Questions

Write down the subjects you want to discuss, such as career, relationships, finances, health, or spiritual practice. Focused questions make the appointment more useful.

## Request an Appointment

Open the consultant directory, choose a profile, and enter your preferred date, preferred time, phone number, and notes. After you submit the request, you can track it under My Consultation Bookings.

## During the Consultation

- Be open and accurate when sharing relevant details.
- Keep your questions and birth information nearby.
- Take notes and ask for clarification when needed.
- Avoid distractions during the scheduled appointment.

## After the Consultation

Review your notes, follow agreed recommendations consistently, and request another appointment when you need follow-up guidance.
