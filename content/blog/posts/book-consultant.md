## Request an appointment

Open **Consult**, choose a consultant, and review the profile. Enter your preferred date, time, phone number, and a short note about the guidance you need.

Select **Request appointment**. The request appears under **Dashboard > My Consultation Bookings** while the consultant reviews it.

## Confirmation

A requested appointment is not a live call or message room. The consultant confirms the schedule before the consultation takes place. Contact support if you need to change the request.
