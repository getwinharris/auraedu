Aura Medical frames its programmes as **job-oriented**, pairing theory with hospital and therapy practice so graduates leave practice-ready. Common pathways include:

## Clinical practice

Work in electropathy clinics and acupuncture practice, or set up your own centre after meeting the required training.

## Hospital & allied health

Join hospital and allied-health teams, drawing on the clinical rotation completed during study.

## Teaching & skilling

Move into teaching, training, and healthcare-skilling roles within the institute network.

The right pathway depends on the course you choose and your goals. The admissions desk can map a programme to your career plan — enquire through the contact page.
