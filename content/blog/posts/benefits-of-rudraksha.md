**Rudraksha** beads have been revered in Hindu spirituality for thousands of years. Derived from the seeds of the *Elaeocarpus ganitrus* tree, these sacred beads are believed to be the tears of Lord Shiva, carrying immense spiritual and healing properties.

## Spiritual Benefits of Rudraksha

- **Meditation aid**: Rudraksha beads help focus the mind and deepen meditation practice
- **Protection**: Creates a protective energy shield against negative influences
- **Chakra balancing**: Each Mukhi corresponds to specific chakras and energy centers
- **Stress relief**: Known to calm the mind and reduce anxiety naturally
- **Spiritual growth**: Accelerates spiritual evolution and self-awareness

## Health Benefits

Modern research has validated many traditional claims about Rudraksha:

- **Blood pressure**: Helps regulate hypertension naturally
- **Heart health**: Strengthens cardiac function
- **Nervous system**: Calms the nervous system and improves sleep quality
- **Immunity**: Boosts overall immunity and vitality
- **Brain function**: Enhances concentration, memory, and mental clarity

## Types of Rudraksha by Mukhi (Faces)

- **1 Mukhi**: Represents the ultimate truth — connects you with divine consciousness
- **2 Mukhi**: Represents Ardhanareeshwara (Shiva-Shakti) — harmonizes relationships
- **3 Mukhi**: Represents Agni (fire) — burns negative karma
- **4 Mukhi**: Represents Brahma — enhances creativity and knowledge
- **5 Mukhi**: The most common — represents Kalagni Rudra — for overall wellbeing
- **6 Mukhi**: Represents Kartikeya — improves focus and decision-making
- **7 Mukhi**: Represents Sapta Rishi — brings wealth and prosperity
- **8 Mukhi**: Represents Vasus — removes obstacles
- **9 Mukhi**: Represents Durga — gives strength and courage
- **10 Mukhi**: Represents Vishnu — protects from negative energies
- **11 Mukhi**: Represents Rudra — grants spiritual powers
- **12 Mukhi**: Represents Surya (Sun) — enhances leadership and authority
- **13 Mukhi**: Represents Kamadeva — attracts love and harmony
- **14 Mukhi**: Represents Hanuman — provides protection and courage

## How to Wear Rudraksha

- **Cleanse**: Soak in clean water for 2-3 hours before first use
- **Energize**: Chant "Om Namah Shivaya" 108 times while holding the bead
- **String**: Use a red or black thread, or a copper/silver chain
- **Wear**: Around the neck, on the right wrist, or as a mala
- **Maintain**: Remove before bathing with soap; wipe with a clean cloth
