The **Navagraha** (nine planets) are celestial deities that influence every aspect of human life according to Vedic astrology. When a planet is weak or afflicted in your birth chart, it can create challenges in specific areas of life. The good news is that Vedic wisdom offers powerful remedies to balance each planet's energy.

## Surya (Sun) Remedies

**Rules**: Soul, authority, health, father
**Weakness signs**: Low confidence, health issues, father-related challenges
- **Mantra**: "Om Hram Hreem Hroum Sah Suryaya Namah" (7,000 times)
- **Gemstone**: Ruby (Manikya) — 5-6 carats in gold ring, worn on ring finger
- **Donation**: Wheat, red cloth, jaggery on Sundays
- **Fasting**: Observe on Sundays — eat only one meal before sunset
- **Food**: Avoid salt on Sundays; donate to temples

## Chandra (Moon) Remedies

**Rules**: Mind, emotions, mother, peace
**Weakness signs**: Anxiety, mood swings, insomnia, mother-related issues
- **Mantra**: "Om Shram Shreem Shroum Sah Chandraya Namah" (11,000 times)
- **Gemstone**: Pearl (Moti) — in silver, worn on little finger
- **Donation**: Milk, curd, white cloth, rice on Mondays
- **Fasting**: Observe on Mondays — consume only milk and fruits
- **Water**: Offer water to the Moon on full moon nights

## Mangala (Mars) Remedies

**Rules**: Energy, courage, siblings, property
**Weakness signs**: Anger, accidents, conflicts, property disputes
- **Mantra**: "Om Kram Kreem Kroum Sah Bhaumaya Namah" (10,000 times)
- **Gemstone**: Red Coral (Moonga) — in gold, worn on ring finger
- **Donation**: Red lentils, wheat, red cloth on Tuesdays
- **Fasting**: Observe on Tuesdays — avoid sour foods
- **Service**: Serve Hanuman temples; chant Hanuman Chalisa

## Budha (Mercury) Remedies

**Rules**: Intellect, communication, business, education
**Weakness signs**: Speech problems, poor memory, business losses
- **Mantra**: "Om Bram Breem Broom Sah Budhaya Namah" (9,000 times)
- **Gemstone**: Emerald (Panna) — in gold, worn on little finger
- **Donation**: Green cloth, moong dal, vegetables on Wednesdays
- **Fasting**: Observe on Wednesdays — eat only once
- **Education**: Teach someone a skill or sponsor a student's education

## Brihaspati (Jupiter) Remedies

**Rules**: Wisdom, knowledge, children, fortune
**Weakness signs**: Lack of wisdom, child-related issues, financial struggles
- **Mantra**: "Om Gram Greem Groum Sah Gurave Namah" (19,000 times)
- **Gemstone**: Yellow Sapphire (Pukhraj) — in gold, worn on index finger
- **Donation**: Yellow cloth, turmeric, chickpeas, gold on Thursdays
- **Fasting**: Observe on Thursdays — avoid salt
- **Worship**: Visit Vishnu or Dattatreya temples

## Shukra (Venus) Remedies

**Rules**: Love, relationships, luxury, arts
**Weakness signs**: Relationship problems, financial instability, lack of creativity
- **Mantra**: "Om Dram Dreem Droum Sah Shukraya Namah" (16,000 times)
- **Gemstone**: Diamond (Heera) — in platinum or silver, worn on middle finger
- **Donation**: White cloth, cow's milk products, silver on Fridays
- **Fasting**: Observe on Fridays — eat only once, avoid spicy food
- **Service**: Donate to women's charities or arts education

## Shani (Saturn) Remedies

**Rules**: Discipline, career, longevity, service
**Weakness signs**: Delays, chronic illness, depression, career obstacles
- **Mantra**: "Om Pram Preem Proum Sah Shanaye Namah" (23,000 times)
- **Gemstone**: Blue Sapphire (Neelam) — in silver or iron, worn on middle finger
- **Donation**: Black cloth, black sesame seeds, iron items on Saturdays
- **Fasting**: Observe on Saturdays — eat only once after sunset
- **Service**: Serve the elderly and poor; feed crows and dogs

## Rahu Remedies

**Rules**: Ambition, illusion, foreign connections, technology
**Weakness signs**: Confusion, addiction, legal issues, relationship drama
- **Mantra**: "Om Bhram Bhreem Bhroum Sah Rahave Namah" (18,000 times)
- **Gemstone**: Hessonite (Gomed) — in silver or panchdhatu, worn on middle finger
- **Donation**: Blue cloth, coconuts, blankets on Saturdays
- **Fasting**: Observe on Saturdays — avoid stale food
- **Service**: Donate to mental health charities

## Ketu Remedies

**Rules**: Spirituality, detachment, past karma, liberation
**Weakness signs**: Confusion, lack of direction, sudden losses, sleep issues
- **Mantra**: "Om Stram Streem Stroum Sah Ketave Namah" (17,000 times)
- **Gemstone**: Cat's Eye (Vaidooryam) — in silver or panchdhatu, worn on middle finger
- **Donation**: Multi-colored cloth, dog food, blankets on Tuesdays
- **Fasting**: Observe on Tuesdays or new moon days
- **Service**: Feed stray animals; volunteer at spiritual organizations

## General Navagraha Remedy

For overall planetary balance, perform the **Navagraha Shanti Pooja** during auspicious timings. You can also chant the **Navagraha Stotram** daily for protection and harmony from all nine planets.
