Electropathy is a system of electro-medical practice that combines drugless therapy with modern clinical assessment. At Aura Medical Institute of Electropathy and Hospital, the **B.E.M.S. (Bachelor of Electro-Medical Sciences)** programme is built around classroom theory, hospital rotation, and hands-on therapy practice.

## What you study

- **Foundations:** Anatomy, Physiology, and Pharmacy in the early years.
- **Core electropathy:** Electropathy Materia Medica, Electrotherapy, and the Practice of Medicine.
- **Clinical subjects:** Pathology, Gynecology, Pediatrics, and Surgery.
- **Acupuncture:** Integrated as part of the allied-therapy training.

## Why it is practice-led

Every student completes a **compulsory hospital internship** before certification. Learning happens where care is delivered, so graduates leave with clinical exposure rather than theory alone.

## Admissions

B.E.M.S. admissions are open with **no NEET requirement and no upper age bar**. Enquire through the contact form or visit the campus at Saravanampatti, Coimbatore to request the prospectus.
