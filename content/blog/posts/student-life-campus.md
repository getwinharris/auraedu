Aura Medical is a co-educational institute where learning and patient care grow together. Student life centres on three things: **training, hospital exposure, and community**.

## Hospital training

Students rotate through the attached hospital as part of their programme, learning care where it is delivered. This is a required part of the B.E.M.S. internship.

## Hostels

Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls support outstation students, with placement assistance for those planning healthcare careers after graduation.

## A learning community

Faculty, clinicians, and students form a community rooted in Coimbatore. Campus tours can be arranged through the admissions desk.
